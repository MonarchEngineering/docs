## Overview

[Zapier](https://zapier.com) is a no-code automation tool that connects your favorite apps into automated workflows. After you **deploy** your Agent, you can trigger it directly from Zapier and use the results in your Zaps.

## Run your deployed Agent from Zapier

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open connection settings">
    Navigate to your Agent's build page and click **Connect**, then select **Zapier** from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the Airtop action">
    In your Zapier recipe (Zap), add the action **Run an Airtop agent** from the Airtop integration.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/connections/zapier-add-airtop.gif"
        alt="Add Airtop action in Zapier"
        style={{ width:"100%" }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your agent">
    In the **Agent** dropdown, select the agent you want to use. If you don't see an agent listed, check the Airtop portal to ensure it is enabled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Agent parameters">
    After selecting the agent, the necessary paratemers will be loaded and displayed automatically. Configure the values accordingly. _Remember to check the field's tooltips for things like field constraints and formats_.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/connections/zapier-set-agent-fields.gif"
        alt="Set Agent parameters"
        style={{ width:"100%" }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the step and save">
    Test the step to ensure the connection works, then save your Zap.
  </Step>
</Steps>
